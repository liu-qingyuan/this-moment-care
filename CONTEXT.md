# This Moment domain context

## Purpose

This Moment is a responsive interaction prototype for people receiving end-of-life care. It helps users understand information, organize thoughts, and prepare communication while preserving user control.

## Boundaries

- The product explains, organizes, and guides.
- It does not diagnose, predict prognosis, recommend treatment, or replace clinicians or counselors.
- Sensitive health and emotional content remains local by default.
- Generating, saving, copying, and sharing always require preview and explicit confirmation.
- Crisis signals interrupt the ordinary generation flow and direct the user to immediate human or emergency support.

## Shared language

- **Original information**: words entered or recorded by the user without AI interpretation.
- **Plain-language explanation**: a simpler restatement that remains distinct from original information.
- **Uncertain information**: content that cannot be established from the user's input.
- **Confirmation question**: a question the user may take to the responsible care team.
- **Expression draft**: user-confirmed wording prepared for a chosen audience and format.
- **Important matters**: people, stories, regrets, gratitude, beliefs, and words the user wishes to preserve.
