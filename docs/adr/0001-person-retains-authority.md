# The person retains interaction authority

This prototype treats the person receiving end-of-life care as its sole decision-making actor. Family members, friends, caregivers, and clinicians may be conversation audiences, and someone may provide physical or accessibility assistance, but the prototype does not provide proxy identities, delegated confirmation, or actions performed on the person's behalf; adding those roles would require a separate privacy, consent, and safety design.
